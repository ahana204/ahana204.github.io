I am currently a 4th Year Doctoral Student, in the Machine Teaching Group at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems. I am advised by Dr. Adish Singla. I am also a student in the Microsoft Research EMEA PhD Scholarship Program (2019).

I am working towards building AI-based assistive systems to aid students in problem-solving with special focus on Computer Science education (https://www.blockstopython.com/). Our problems are modelled using techniques from program synthesis, sequential decision making and reinforcment learning.

I am currently also leading BITSAA International's Pulse team, overseeing a YouTube-based podcast initiative affiliated with BITSAA, the alumni association of BITS Pilani.
